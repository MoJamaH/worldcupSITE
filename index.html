Welcome to the World Cup!

The World Cup is the most prestigious international football tournament, held every four years. Every country in the world is eligible to compete for the coveted title of World Cup Champion.

The World Cup is a tournament of 32 teams that compete in group stages and elimination rounds. Each team plays each other in their group, and the two best teams move on to the next round. The tournament culminates in the final game, where the two remaining teams battle it out for the title.
